ABOUT THE PROJECT

Churn Risk :


Churn risk is the likelihood that a customer will cancel their subscription. A customer that is a high churn-risk is very likely to cancel


Impact of Churn Prediction :


The ability to predict churn before it happens allows businesses to take proactive actions to keep existing customers from churning.

This could look like: Customer success teams reaching out to those high-risk customers to provide support or to gauge what needs may not be being met.
